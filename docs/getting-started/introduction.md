# Getting Started with Extension Monetization

Welcome to the Extension Monetization Playbook! This guide will help you understand how to effectively monetize your browser extension.

## Quick Start

1. **Understand Your Model**: Choose a monetization model that fits your extension:
   - Freemium: Basic features free, premium features paid
   - Subscription: Recurring payments for ongoing value
   - One-time Purchase: Pay once, own forever
   - Affiliate: Earn commissions from referrals

2. **Set Up Payments**: Configure your payment processor:
   - Stripe is the recommended choice for extensions
   - Chrome Web Store Payments for simple transactions

3. **Protect Your Revenue**: Implement license key validation or server-side checks

## Choosing Your Monetization Model

### Consider These Factors

- **Target Audience**: B2B vs B2C has different pricing expectations
- **Update Frequency**: SaaS-like extensions work well with subscriptions
- **Value Delivery**: Continuous value = subscription; one-time value = purchase
- **Competition**: Research similar extensions and their pricing
